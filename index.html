About Me

Hello friends...
I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Dr. Daniela Korčákova in the Astronomical Institute of Charles University, Prague. I'm interested in the B[e] phenomenon, or in other words, hot (B-type) stars with regions of turbulent and chaotic circumstellar matter, and I am at the moment working on radiative transfer models of their dusty environments with the code HDUST. I completed my master's degree in astrophysics at the University of Galway under the supervision of Dr. Christian Ginski in 2025, in which I was examining circumstellar matter for an entirely different reason - probing the development of early planetary systems!
